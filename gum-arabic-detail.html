Gum Arabic

We supply high-quality Gum Arabic, a natural gum harvested from the hardened sap of the acacia tree. This versatile product is a cornerstone of various industries, valued for its unique properties as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and thickening agent.

Our Gum Arabic is sustainably sourced and processed to ensure purity and performance, making it a reliable choice for your production needs.

Industrial Applications:

  • Food Industry: Used as a stabilizer in soft drinks, a thickener in candies and marshmallows, and an emulsifier in flavorings.
  • Pharmaceuticals: Acts as a binder in tablets and a soothing agent in syrups.
  • Paints and Inks: Used as a binder in watercolor paints and a viscosity controller in inks.
  • Cosmetics: Functions as an adhesive in masks and other cosmetic products.
